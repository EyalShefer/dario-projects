# Captain Count Global Launch — Design Document

**Date:** February 26, 2026  
**Project:** Captain Count (Educational Math Game for Kids)  
**Status:** APPROVED

---

## Executive Summary

Captain Count is a high-quality educational math game for grades K-2 (ages 4-7), featuring 70 mini-games that cover the grade 1 curriculum. Launching globally with a tiered freemium model, starting in Brazil.

---

## Market Analysis

### Global Opportunity
- **Kids' Educational Games Market:** $20.58B by 2030 (CAGR 22.59%)
- **Target Segment:** Grade 1 math education
- **TAM:** 300M+ children globally

### Launch Markets (Prioritized)
1. **Brazil (Portuguese)** — 210M market, high EdTech adoption, price-sensitive
2. **Spanish-speaking (ES)** — 500M+ market, strong EdTech growth
3. **English-speaking (EN)** — 400M+ market, high ARPU ($5-8/mo)

### Market Sizing (Year 1)
- **Conservative:** 50K-100K users, $5-10K/month revenue
- **Moderate:** 100K-500K users, $20-50K/month revenue
- **Aggressive:** 500K-1M users, $50-100K/month revenue

---

## Product Design

### Features
- **70 mini-games** covering grade 1 math curriculum
- **High-quality graphics** (differentiator vs competitors)
- **Progression system** (unlock games as student masters concepts)
- **Parent dashboard** (optional: track child progress)
- **Multilingual support** (4+ languages day 1)

### User Experience
- Target: 4-7 year olds (K-2 grades)
- Session length: 4-5 minutes per game
- Daily engagement goal: 10-15 min/day
- Progression: 6+ months of content

---

## Monetization Strategy

### Tiered Freemium Model

| Tier | Price (BRL) | Price (USD) | Content | Target |
|------|-----------|-----------|---------|--------|
| **Free** | R$0 | Free | 5-7 games + ads | Acquisition hook |
| **Basic** | R$9.99/mo | ~$2/mo | 30 games, no ads | Price-sensitive parents |
| **Premium** | R$19.99/mo | ~$4/mo | All 70 games + cosmetics | Engaged families |
| **Annual** | R$99/year | ~$20/year | All features + discount | Committed users |

### Revenue Model
- **Primary:** Subscription (Basic + Premium)
- **Secondary:** Optional cosmetics (character skins, themes)
- **Tertiary:** Ads (free tier only)

### Projected ARPU
- **Conservative:** R$2-3/user/month (~$0.40-0.60)
- **Target:** R$3-5/user/month (~$0.60-1.00)
- **Optimistic:** R$5-8/user/month (~$1.00-1.60)

## Success Metrics (KPIs)

### User Acquisition
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** R$2-3 per user (Facebook ads)
- **Organic conversion rate:** 2-5% (free → paid)
- **Paid conversion rate:** 5-10% (with optimization)

### Engagement
- **DAU (Daily Active Users):** 25-30% of installed base
- **D7 Retention:** 25%+ (realistic for education games)
- **D30 Retention:** 15%+
- **Session length:** 10-15 min/session

### Monetization
- **ARPU:** R$3-5/month (all segments)
- **Conversion (Free → Paid):** 2-3% within 7 days
- **Payback period:** 45-60 days
- **LTV (Lifetime Value):** 180+ days (6 months+)

## Launch Timeline

### Phase 1: Brazil (Months 1-3)
**Week 1-2:** Soft Launch (Organic Only)
- Google Play release (Portuguese)
- App Store optimization (ASO)
- Target: 500-1K downloads
- Monitor: Retention, ARPU, crashes

**Week 3-4:** Early Scale
- Small Facebook ads ($500-1K budget)
- Community engagement (Reddit, parenting blogs)
- Target: 3-5K active users
- Iterate: Pricing, messaging, onboarding

**Months 2-3:** Optimization
- Scale ads based on LTV/CAC ratio
- A/B test pricing tiers
- Target: 10K-20K active users

### Phase 2: Spanish Markets (Months 3-6)
- Launch: Spanish version (Spain + LATAM)
- Leverage Brazil learnings
- Target: 50K+ active users combined

### Phase 3: English Markets (Months 6-12)
- Launch: English (US, UK, Australia)
- Higher competition, but higher ARPU
- Target: 100K+ active users

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| **High CAC in Brazil** | Unprofitable acquisition | Start organic, iterate pricing before scaling ads |
| **Low D7 retention** | High churn | A/B test onboarding, difficulty curves |
| **Competition from Duolingo** | Market share loss | Focus on game quality, not just education |
| **Regulatory (GDPR, COPPA)** | Legal risk | Use privacy-first analytics, parental consent |
| **Platform policy changes** | Revenue impact | Diversify to multiple app stores, web version |
