# Areas

**Ongoing areas of responsibility and interest.**

Use this folder for areas that require regular attention but don't have a specific end date. These are roles or domains you maintain over time.

- Health & fitness
- Personal finance
- Professional development
- Relationships
- Hobbies & interests
- Work & career
- Home & environment

## Format

Create a folder for each area:
```
areas/
  area-name/
    README.md        (description, current focus)
    standards.md     (principles, habits, goals)
    notes.md         (ongoing notes, reflections)
```

## Characteristics

- **Ongoing:** No fixed deadline
- **Owned:** You're responsible for maintaining progress
- **Measurable:** Track via metrics, habits, or periodic reviews

## Review Cycle

Review areas monthly or quarterly to assess health and adjust focus.
